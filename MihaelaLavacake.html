Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Unsalted butter 1 stick (4 ounces)
Bittersweet chocolate, preferably Valrhona 6 ounces
Eggs 2
Egg yolks 2
Sugar 1/4 cup
Kosher salt 1 pinch
All-purpose flour 2 tablespoons, plus more for dusting the ramekins
Love As much as possible

Info Box

  • Preparation Time: 10 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 35 minutes
  • Serving Time: 5 minutes
  • Resting Time: 15 minutes
  • Nutrition Score: 25/10

Molten Chocolate Cake

Molten chocolate cake became famous for a reason: It's cakey on the outside with an irresistible center of flowing warm dark chocolate. The first time you try one, it may seem like magic, but this chocolate molten cake recipe is actually quite easy to make and ready in under an hour. Be sure to use the finest quality chocolate — it will make a big difference. These gooey, rich cakes are enough on their own, but you can serve them with sweetened whipped cream or vanilla ice cream if you like.

Step 1
Gather the ingredients.

Step 2
Preheat oven to 450°F. Butter and lightly flour four 6-ounce ramekins. Tap out excess flour. Set the ramekins on a baking sheet.

Step 3
In a double boiler, over simmering water, melt butter with chocolate.

Step 4
In a medium bowl, beat eggs with egg yolks, sugar, and salt at high speed until thickened and pale.

Step 5
Whisk melted chocolate until smooth.

Step 6
Quickly fold it into egg mixture, along with flour.

Step 7
Spoon batter into prepared ramekins and bake for 12 minutes, or until sides of cakes are firm but centers are soft.

Step 8
Let cakes cool in ramekins for 1 minute, then cover each with an inverted dessert plate. Carefully turn each cake over, let stand for 10 seconds, and then unmold. Serve immediately.
