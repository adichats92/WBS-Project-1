Step 1 - Gather the ingredients
Step 2 - Preheat oven to 450°F. Butter and lightly flour four 6-ounce ramekins. Tap out excess flour. Set the ramekins on a baking sheet.
Step 3 - In a double boiler, over simmering water, melt butter with chocolate
Step 4 - In a medium bowl, beat eggs with egg yolks, sugar, and salt at high speed until thickened and pale.
Step 5 - Whisk melted chocolate until smooth.
Step 6 - Quickly fold it into egg mixture, along with flour.
Step 7 - Spoon batter into prepared ramekins and bake for 12 minutes, or until sides of cakes are firm but centers are soft.
Step 8 - Let cakes cool in ramekins for 1 minute, then cover each with an inverted dessert plate. Carefully turn each cake over, let stand for 10 seconds, and then unmold. Serve immediately.
